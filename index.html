The Concept

YEOYU is a third-space café rooted in the Korean concept of 여유—creating room in time and space for people to work, rest, and connect without pressure.

Journal

Why 여유 Matters

Spring 2026

In a city defined by urgency, 여유 offers something rare: room. Not just physical space, but the ability to exist without constant pressure to move, produce, or leave.

YEOYU is designed as a response to that condition. A place where time is not tightly held, and where being around others does not demand interaction, but allows it to happen naturally.

It is not about escaping the city, but reshaping how we move within it—together, more slowly.
